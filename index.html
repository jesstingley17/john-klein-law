About Jonathan W. Klein

With over 28 years of legal experience, Jonathan W. Klein has established himself as a trusted advocate for clients throughout Columbus, Ohio and the surrounding areas.

Based in Powell, Ohio, Attorney Klein brings a wealth of knowledge and a commitment to excellence in every case. His practice encompasses a wide range of legal matters, ensuring comprehensive representation for individuals and families facing complex legal challenges.

Known for his meticulous attention to detail and unwavering dedication to his clients, Jonathan W. Klein combines legal expertise with compassionate service, providing personalized solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.
